Chicken Stew

Cooking Procedures

As always, I prepare my ingredients to make my cooking easy and fast.

Ingredients

  • 10 Big-sized fresh tomatoes
  • Bell pepper
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Green pepper
  • Groundnut oil
  • Curry leaf
  • Chicken
  • Curry masala mixture
  • Ginger and Garlic(fresh)

Steps

  1. Pour groundnut oil into already heated pot.
  2. Add your blended mix- tomatoes, curry leaf, ginger, garlic, cayenne, bell pepper and onions. Stir at intervals to avoid burn until sour taste goes away.
  3. Put your sliced green pepper to fry
  4. Now add the fried chicken, chicken stock. Also add your crayfish and seasioning cubes too.
  5. Finally, add to taste. Your delicious chicken stew is ready to go down a bowl of rice and chilled wine.
