Egusi soup

Cooking Procedures

I like preparing my ingredients- chopping the onions, cooking the meat and fishes together, blending my pepper, slicing my ukazi leaf etc.

Ingredients

  • Red-oil
  • Ground crayfish
  • Fish, meat, stockfish
  • Ground Egusi
  • Ukasi leaf
  • Fresh pepper
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Add sizable quantity of red-oil into your pot and allow to heat over medium heat.
  2. Pour in your blended pepper and chopped onions, little water depending on the size of soup and stir well a few moments.
  3. Pour the stock from the meat.
  4. Add your ground egusi and cook for a minute.
  5. Then add your meat, fish and stockfish. Cover your pot to simmer.
  6. Now put your ground crayfish, seasoning cubes and salt to taste and stir well.
  7. finally, add your ukazi leaf -to give your that divine flavour and taste - and stir.
  8. Your sumptous egusi soup is ready to eat with your favorite swallow!
