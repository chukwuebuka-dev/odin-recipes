Beans

Cooking procedures

Ingredients

  • Red-oil
  • Beans(white preferrably)
  • Fresh pepper
  • Ground crayfish
  • Salt
  • 4 Big-sized tomatoes
  • Smoked fish

Steps

  1. Gleen your beans depending on the quantity you wish cook- to remove stones, weevils and husks.
  2. Wash well in a bowl of salted lukewarm water and transfer into a pot of already boiling water.
  3. Add some slices of onions and salt to taste and allow to boil until very soft.
  4. In another dry pot, heat red-oil, add your blended mixture of pepper, tomatoes etc., stir and fry for 3mins.
  5. Add your powdered crayfish, smoked fish, little water and salt to taste. Then strain your cooked beans add to the mix.
  6. stir well and allow to simmer a little under low heat.
  7. Your porridge beans is ready to be served. Yummy!
